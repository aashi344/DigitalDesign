  Air Quality  

As cities grow, air quality often worsens, affecting the health of those who live there. Pollution from traffic, industry, and daily activities like waste disposal and energy use makes breathing harder and increases health risks. The way we live in urban spaces—how we get around, what we consume, and how we manage waste—directly shapes the air we breathe and our overall well-being.
